ABOUT THE COMPANY

Branding Idea

What does the company do or offer?

The company offers modern day music and film presented in a manner to resemble the market in the mid to late 30th century. Examples of products include vinyl records, cassette tapes and VHS tapes.

Who is the company to potential customers

The targetted demographic are youth who share an interest in music and enjoy the aesthetic of the products for it mixes modern with vintage.

Why might the company entire someone to purchase a product?

Someone may be interested in purchasing a product for this may be their way of being introduced or re-introduced to listening to music through classic forms of entertainment.
