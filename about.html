Our mission is to offer modern day music that is presented in a manner that resembles the market in the mid to late 30th century. We provide a place where people can purchase collectable products such as vinyl records, cassette tapes and more. The targetted demographic of REWIND are youth who share an interest in music and enjoy the aesthetic of the products for it mixes modern with vintage.

We were established in Vancouver, BC as a small digital shop. Founded by Newton Nguyen-Le and Patrick Pacunana.
